Welcome to Bloom & Brew, where coffee meets artistry and baking is a floral affair. Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, our cozy café and bakery is a celebration of bold flavors and delicate blooms. From lavender-laced lattes to rose-infused macarons, our menu is inspired by nature’s most aromatic treasures, offering a sensory escape with every sip and bite. We are passionate about creating unique experiences that blend the warmth of fresh coffee with the elegance of floral notes.

At Bloom & Brew, our mission is to craft moments that delight the senses and nourish the soul. We source the finest ingredients, blending sustainable, locally roasted coffee with handpicked edible flowers to create a menu that is as vibrant as it is delicious. Whether you're here for a freshly baked hibiscus scone, a jasmine tea-infused cheesecake, or a quiet corner to unwind, we invite you to enjoy the delicate harmony of blooms and brews.
